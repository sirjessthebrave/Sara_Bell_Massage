ABOUT ME

Hello! Welcome to my website! I am Sara and I was born and raised in California. After living in a variety of places from as far north as Oregon and as far south as Santa Cruz, my heart brought me back to Humboldt County. I originally started school at Humboldt State to study kinesiology when I became interested in massage and bodywork. I attended Arcata School of Massage and graduated in 2012. During that time I also trained to become a teacher in both Mat and Reformer Stott Pilates with Ineka at Mindful Movement Collective. I instantly fell in love with bodywork and the art of healing and wellness. I continue my education whenever possible by doing Pilates workshops and advanced massage technique classes. I now also teach Postural Analysis at Arcata School of Massage. The more I learn, the more I continue to combine a wide range of techniques to find the best way for each individual client to reach optimal health and body awareness. I find through anatomy, reflexology, pathology, physiology, energetic and lymphatic flow, Swedish massage techniques, myofascial release, trigger point therapy, shiatsu and thai yoga massage fundamentals and Pilates principles along with a dialogue with each individual client we can work together to create a unique treatment plan to reach optimal health and happiness.

Sara has been doing a variety of bodywork all over Humboldt since graduating from Arcata School of Massage in 2012. While in massage school she became interested in Pilates and trained for teaching both Mat and Reformer Pilates with Ineka at Mindful Movement Collective. She has continued diversifying her knowledge and techniques with advanced and supplemental courses in both Pilates and Massage. She also teaches Postural Analysis both at Arcata School of Massage and in her private studio. On her free time she enjoys hiking, camping, participating in local community theatre, dancing, snowboarding, wakeboarding and spending time with friends, family and most importantly her god, Rita, and cat, Snickers.
